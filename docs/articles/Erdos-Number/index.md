# My Erdős number: 3

The [Erdős number](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erd%C5%91s_number) describes the collaborative distance between mathematician Paul Erdős and another person, as measured by authorship of mathematical papers. Here's my connection:

## 1. Åke Edlund → A. Nitzan

U. Peskin, Å. Edlund, I. Bar-On, M. Galperin & A. Nitzan,  
*Transient Resonance Structures in Electron Tunneling Through Water*,  
J. Chem. Phys. **111**, 7558–7566 (1999)

## 2. A. Nitzan → Peter Salamon

B. Andresen, R. S. Berry, A. Nitzan & P. Salamon,  
*Thermodynamics in Finite Time I: The Step Carnot Cycle*,  
Phys. Rev. A **15**, 2086–2093 (1977)

## 3. Peter Salamon → Paul Erdős

P. Salamon & P. Erdős,  
*The Solution to a Problem of Grünbaum*,  
Canadian Math. Bulletin **31**, 129–138 (1988)
